Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 2 5G

Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 2 smartphone was launched on 1st September 2020. The phone comes with a 7.60-inch touchscreen primary display with a resolution of 1768x2208 pixels. It also features a 6.20-inch touchscreen as its second display, with a resolution of 816x2260 pixels. Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 2 is powered by an octa-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 865+ processor that features 1 core clocked at 3.09GHz, 3 cores clocked at 2.4GHz and 4 cores clocked at 1.8GHz. It comes with 12GB of RAM. The Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 2 runs Android 10 and is powered by a 4500mAh battery. The Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 2 supports wireless charging, as well as proprietary fast charging.
